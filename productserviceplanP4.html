Product/Service Plan


Below is the production process and controls Comfort Crate will employ in creating our self-care kits:


A. Preparation for the Self-Care Kits

  1. Items belonging in the three specified sets will first be sourced from the shops and stores previously mentioned in the Material, Equipment Requirements, and Sources of Supply section.


  2. Quality control will be done to ensure that the content of each set is in good condition.


    1. For food items in the snacks set, expiration dates will be checked to ensure the safety of our customers. The same will be applied for the sheet masks, eye masks, and katinko rub that are included in the relaxation set.

    2. For other non-food items, their overall appearance and functionality (especially for the electric massagers found in the relaxation set) will be examined to ensure a consistent quality on the inside and outside portion of each content.

    3. Each item will also be sanitized.


  3. Once the items have been acquired and inspected, they are now ready to be assembled into their respective packages
