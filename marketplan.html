Market Analysis

Presented in the table are five enterprises under the self-care packaging business on the online selling platform Shopee:


The table displays the monthly demand, supply, total demand, and total supply of self-care packages that these shops offer. The most common set sold by these brands is the Snacks Set. It can be concluded based on their sales or number of items sold that the top two most in demand sets are the Snacks Set and Letters (Stationery) Set. Furthermore, shops who offer one or both of the aforementioned sets have also shown to have greater sales. This indicates that when customers buy self-care packages, they usually look for food items or stationery items, which fall directly under the snacks and letters category. Despite coming in third, the demand for the relaxation set does not fall behind and sometimes even surpass the total sales of the other sets as seen in certain brands. Personalized add-ons also show promising potential when it comes to the total number of items sold.
