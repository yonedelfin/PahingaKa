3.6 Distribution Logistics

Comfort Crate will initially operate home-based and use social media sites (e.g. Facebook, Twitter, Instagram) and online shopping sites (e.g. Lazada, Shopee) to display our self-care kits for ease of browsing and ordering as well as to reach a wider range of audience. Customers will have their packages delivered to their chosen address with their chosen courier (J&T or Lalamove) and they have the option to pay through either cash on delivery (COD) or through E-wallets (e.g. GCash, PayMaya).
