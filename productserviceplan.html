Product/Service Plan


Purpose of the Product

Our products seek to aid individuals interested in self-care for themselves and their loved ones by providing specially curated and customizable self-care sets that can not only alleviate stress and anxiety, but also provide relaxation and happiness.


Product's Unique Features

Our self-care packages can adapt to the customers' demands as they come in three different specific sets (Snacks, Stationery, and Relaxation Sets) created to serve the various needs of the buyers. In addition, we offer a customizable set that allows the customers to include their own personal touch into the self-care package. Buyers can also choose from our personalized add-ons such as handcrafted mini-bouquets, playlist cards, and polaroid photos to make their packages extra special (even more so if they wish to gift it to a special someone).
