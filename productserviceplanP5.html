Product/Service Plan


B. Assembling the Self-Care Kits

  1. A pastel pink mailer box will be utilized as the main package for the self-care kits. Before arranging the content of the packages, layers of Japanese paper will first be placed onto the box, followed by shredded paper to make sure that the contents stay secure and safe as much as possible during delivery.

  2. The contents of each set are as follows:

    1. The Snacks Set contains one (1) drink, one (1) mini-chocolate, 3 pieces each of two (2) different types of candies (picked from 3 options), one (1) small snack, one (1) big snack, one (1) big chips, and 5 pieces of one (1) small chips (picked from 2 options).

    2. The Stationery Set, also known as Letters Set, will contain three (3) highlighter pens, five (5) washi tapes, two (2) gel pens, sticky notes, notepads, stickers, a mini journal, and a mini desk calendar.

    3. The Relaxation Set will contain a katinko rub, two (2) sheet masks, two (2) eye masks, a sleep mask, a mini-electric massager, and a squishy toy.

    4. The Customized Set will depend on the items customers will add to their carts.

  3. Once the package has been assembled, a box sleeve containing the set label and company logo will be used to keep the box from opening. It will be further secured with bubble wrap and bubble mailers before handing it out for delivery.

  4. Quality control will also be employed to make sure that the packaging is ideal enough to withstand deep dents, heavy bumps, scratches, and etc. All packaging materials will also be sanitized.
