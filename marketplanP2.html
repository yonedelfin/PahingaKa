4.2 Marketing Strategy

Comfort Crate will employ various marketing strategies that will communicate our productâ€™s main purpose and showcase our special selling points.


Social Media Marketing

Social media has cemented itself as a significant part of our daily lives (even more so with the ongoing pandemic) thus, making it the perfect avenue for our brand to connect to a wider audience. As people become more increasingly aware of the importance of mental health with the help of the internet, we can utilize social media, mainly Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok to promote our products and how we can help contribute to this rising cause.


Content Marketing

In line with our social media marketing, our company plans to promote our products by putting out various content online using a number of creative methods. This includes video advertisements (will be applied heavily on Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram), eye-catching graphic posters and images (will be applied mostly on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram), hashtag and profile frame events people can participate in (will be applied mostly on Twitter and Facebook), interactive story posts (will be applied on Facebook and Instagram), and many more.


Relationship Marketing

Part of our product line includes a customized set as well as the option of having personalized add-ons. This adds to the possibility of having direct contact with our buyers in case they ask for assistance or are in need of further clarifications. As such, our team aims to provide our customers with exemplary customer service, allowing us to build and maintain strong relationships with our buyers.


Communal Marketing

Our company also aims to use the testimonies and feedback we receive from our customers as part of our marketing strategy. Sharing their experiences in how Comfort Crate was able to help in their act of self-care for themselves or their loved ones can help us build a stronger following and attract potential customers along the way.
