4.5 Pricing Policy

Comfort Crate utilized the cost-plus pricing strategy as the basis for setting the pricing policy of our self-care products. This means that the figures are heavily based on the overall sourcing and production costs of the self-care packages and personalized items. A mark-up of at least 30% was added to the total calculated costs of the raw materials and creation process in order to obtain the final selling price presented in the table.
